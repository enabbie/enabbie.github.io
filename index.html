About Me

I am a second-year PhD student at USQ, focusing on multi-planet systems with transit timing variations. I have experience using both photometric and spectroscopic datasets to characterize and detect planets in these systems. My research interests also include ultra-short period planets, with a particular focus on ultrahot Neptunes. I received my Bachelor's degree from the University of Florida.
